Purpose
To create places which contribute positively to whole communities by enhancing; adding to; and using existing local assets, inspiration, and potential, with the purpose of creating new spaces that promote people's health, happiness, wellbeing, and personal growth and reduce social isolation and loneliness.
Means
Employing the power of architectural and design services to support the provision of safe, decent homes and temporary accommodation for all; together with community, healthcare and commercial buildings and facilities in landscape led inclusive public settings designed to promote improved health outcomes, both preventative and rehabilitative, and enhance happiness, joy and wellbeing. We will design spaces which engender a sense of belonging and connection to; and integration with, both the immediate community and the wider community beyond.
